Although your institution does their best for you safety in real life as well as online, you still hold the biggest percentage of responsibility for getting hacked so you need to take measures to ensure your information is not stolen by bad actors

How?

The passwords you use are one of the most important factors in staying safe online

Having your dogs name as your password although cute is very risky, such passwords can be stolen or brute forced easily so Make sure your passwords are complex and include:

  • Uppercase Letters
  • Lowercase Letters
  • Numbers
  • Symbols

    • Tip: Use a password manager
