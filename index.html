Passwords

The passwords you use are one of the most important factors in staying safe online

Having your dogs name as your password although cute is very risky, such passwords can be stolen or brute forced easily so Make sure your passwords are complex and include:

  • Uppercase Letters
  • Lowercase Letters
  • Numbers
  • Symbols

    • Tip: Use a password generator

    Links

    Could lead to malware being installed on your machine

    Dont open a link if you are not sure what it is
    Same goes for files, dont download if you are not sure what you are downloading
    Make sure you trust the source

    Phising

    If its too good to be true, then its probably a scam!

    Dont fall for such, be sure to know to who you are talking and beware of their intentions
    Verify where you put your information and be extra cautious
